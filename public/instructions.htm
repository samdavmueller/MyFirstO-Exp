Instructions for the 'Guess the Urn'-Game

Please read these instructions carefully!


In this game, you have to guess the color of an urn.

There are two versions of this game. In the first version you guess on your own which color different urns have. In the second, you guess the color of the urn together with a group of two other players by voting. You receive information about your performance and bonus payment in the end of the game.

Single-Player version of 'Guess the Urn'

The urn can be blue or red. Both colors are equally likely. You receive signals that can help you make your decision. All signals you receive contain a color, either red or blue. On average, six out of ten signals contain the same color as the urn they are describing, while four signals show a different color. For example, if the urn is blue, there is a % chance that the signal you receive is blue and a % chance that it is red.

You are paid when all your decisions match the true color of the different urns. During the game we use an Experimental Currency (ECU). 10 ECU are equal to $. If you guess the color of all urns correctly, you receive 30 ECU. If you understand the instructions press the 'Done' button to proceed to single player game of 'Guess the Urn'.
