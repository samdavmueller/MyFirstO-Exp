The Bomb Game


In this part of the experiment you will play the bomb game.

Below you see 100 black boxes. In one of these boxes there is a bomb.

You have to decide how many boxes you want to open. Each box there contains 1 ECU.

You will get the sum of all ECU that were in the boxes you opened.

However, if you open the box with the bomb, you get nothing.

How many boxes do you want to open?

Use the slider to change the number of boxes you want to open.
