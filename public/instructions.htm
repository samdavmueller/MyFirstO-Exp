Instructions for the first task

Please read these instructions carefully!


In this task, you have to guess the color of an urn. The urn can be blue or red. Both colors are equally likely.
In each urn there are 100 balls. In the blue urn there are blue balls and red balls. While in the red urn there are red balls and blue balls.
To help you guess the correct color of the urn, one ball is randomly drawn out of the urn, the color shown to you and then ball is returned to the urn. This procedure is possibly repeated several times. Your hint might then look for example as follows:
blue, blue, red
In this task you have to guess which color the urn probably has.
You are paid when all your different guesses match the most likely color of the different urns.
During the game we use an Experimental Currency (ECU). 10 ECU are equal to $. If you guess the color of all urns correctly, you receive 30 ECU.
You receive information about your performance and bonus payment in the end of the game.
If you understand the instructions press the 'Done' button to proceed to the first task.
